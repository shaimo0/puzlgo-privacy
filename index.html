PuzlGo — Privacy Policy

Operator: Northlight Studio ("we", "us").

PuzlGo is a competitive riddle game designed to work with minimal personal data. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices.

We do not ask for your real name, email, phone number, or precise location. Sign-in is anonymous by default — no registration required.

Optional features

Some features are off by default and only process data if enabled in a future update or a specific build:

  • Purchases / Pro subscription — if you buy Pro, purchase and subscription status are processed by Google Play (and RevenueCat) to deliver the entitlement and prevent fraud.
  • Ads — if ads are enabled, Google AdMob may use advertising identifiers to show rewarded ads. Pro removes ads.
  • Product analytics — if configured, pseudonymous usage events (tied to your anonymous id) help us improve the app.

Third parties (processors)

  • Supabase — anonymous auth and database (leaderboard/profiles).
  • Google Play (and RevenueCat, if used) — subscriptions and purchases.
  • Google AdMob — rewarded ads (only when ads are enabled).

Each processes data under its own privacy policy.

Children

PuzlGo is a general-audience trivia game and is not directed at children under 13 (or the age of digital consent in your region).

Your choices & rights

  • Delete your data: email zenrollbingo@gmail.com to delete your profile.
  • Ads: you can limit ad tracking in your device settings; Pro removes ads.
  • Depending on your region (GDPR/CCPA) you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your data — email us to exercise them.

Data retention & security

We keep profile data while your account exists and delete it on request. Data in transit is encrypted (HTTPS/TLS). Ratings and balances are server-authoritative and cannot be edited by the client.

Changes

We'll update this policy and the "Last updated" date; material changes will be surfaced in-app or here.
